Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to Common Questions

From timelines to process details, here are quick answers to the most frequent questions I get.

Contact Me
What's your design process?

My design process typically involves five key phases: Discovery & Research, where I understand your goals and users; Wireframing & Information Architecture, where I map out the structure; UI Design, where I create the visual interface; Prototyping & Testing, where we validate the design; and finally, Development Handoff with detailed documentation.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple landing page or UI design might take 2-3 weeks, while a complete web application with frontend development could take 2-3 months. During our initial consultation, I'll provide a detailed timeline based on your specific requirements.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, most projects are split into 2-3 payments. Typically, I require a deposit to start, a midpoint payment after key milestones, and a final payment upon project completion. We can discuss a payment structure that works for your budget.

Can you also design my website?

Yes! I offer basic web design or collaborate with developers. I can handle the entire process from design to development, or work alongside your development team to ensure the design is implemented perfectly.

What do I need to get started?

Just fill out the contact form and tell me about your project. I'll take it from there.
